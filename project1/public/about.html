OUR VISION

Through our advocacy for the protection and freedom of every whale and dolphin, we take a vital stride in nurturing a harmonious coexistence between humanity and the environment. Our vision propels us toward creating a world where ecological equilibrium and the safeguarding of our planet's climate resilience stand as the foremost priorities in our collective endeavors.
As steadfast stewards of the seas, our commitment goes beyond mere words; it's a call to action for a more sustainable and balanced world. We invite all who share our vision to join us in this noble pursuit, where the safeguarding of marine life and climate resilience intertwine as the cornerstones of our shared responsibility.

OUR VALUE

Passion – We care deeply about whales and dolphins, and we share this passion with our supporters.
Integrity – Our work is backed by robust research, science and philosophy.
Courage – We are not afraid to take on big business, governments or industries whose actions harm whales and dolphins.

WANT TO HELP SAVE ORCA?

Efforts are essential in safeguarding the wild orca population, and with your generous support, we can sustain our mission and mitigate the threats that endanger these magnificent creatures.
Across the globe, numerous orcas, along with other dolphins and whales, are subjected to exploitation in marine parks for entertainment purposes. However, you have the power to make a difference by adopting Springer and her family, thereby contributing to the protection of wild orca.
Your support is a beacon of hope for these incredible marine mammals, offering them a chance at a life free from captivity and exploitation. By adopting Springer and her family, you become an integral part of our mission to ensure that wild orca can flourish in their natural habitats, playing a vital role in maintaining the balance of our oceans.
